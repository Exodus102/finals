About Us

The University of Rizal System (URS) as an organized academic entity composed of separate but interrelated units coordinates and integrates system-wide functions and activities. It has the prime mandate of providing instruction, research, extension and production to the public.

With the Board of Regents as the highest governing board, the University is also led by its president, Dr. Nancy Talavera Pascual. With her, are three vice-presidents – Dr. Renato F. De Lemon, Vice-president for Administration and Finance; Dr. Allan E. Conde, Vice-president for Academic Affairs; and, Dr. Marites M. Rio, Vice-president for Research Development, Extension and Production. The University is also managed by campus directors, unit heads, deans and program heads.

The President is the chief executive officer, university leader and spokesperson. She performs her administrative and leadership work and participates in related activities such as instruction, extension, production and research overseeing all university operations, including programs and policy directions.

URST HISTORY

  The University of Rizal System Taytay Campus was established in 2005 and first resided at Rosario Ocampo Elementary School. The first offered courses were Civil Technology, Bachelor of Secondary Education major in English and General Education, Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurial Management, Call Center Training Program, Two year Civil Technology, and Two-year Garments Technology. During the first batch of students, there were six pioneer instructors who guided and mentored them.
In 2007, URS Taytay was transferred to its new building at the Rizal Technopark and offered the two-year course practical nursing program. In the same year, the campus had its first Campus Dean in the person of Prof. Grenelita D.C. Bilbao, and its first chancellor, Dr. Reneecilia B. Paz De Leon. In 2013, the campus offered BSE English, BEED, BS Psychology, and BS Information Technology due to public demand.
Today, URS Taytay has emerged as the leading provider of health care with Nursing as its flagship program. The URS Taytay community continuously commits to deliver excellent and quality services in order to meet the demands of the globally competitive world.
